About Us

Deliciously Filipino is a family-owned restaurant dedicated to bringing the traditional flavors of the Philippines to your table. We use fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create mouthwatering dishes that will transport you to the beautiful islands of the Philippines.

Our Menu

Explore our wide selection of Filipino dishes, from hearty soups and stews to savory grilled meats and delectable desserts. Whether you're a fan of nilaga, sinigang, or kare-kare, we have something for everyone.

Nilaga

Nilaga is a traditional Filipino boiled soup dish made with tender beef, vegetables, and aromatic spices.

Sinigang

A sour soup with a tamarind base, loaded with vegetables and your choice of meat or seafood.

Kare-Kare

A rich and creamy stew made with oxtail, tripe, or beef, cooked in peanut sauce and served with bagoong.

Sisig

Sisig is a sizzling and savory Filipino dish made from chopped and seasoned pig's head and liver, typically served with rice and condiments.

Bangus

Bangus, also known as milkfish, is a popular and versatile fish in Filipino cuisine, often cooked in various ways such as grilled, fried, or stewed.

Calamares

Calamares is a delicious and crispy Filipino dish made from battered and deep-fried squid rings, typically served with a tangy dipping sauce.

Palabok

Palabok is a Filipino noodle dish with rice noodles, shrimp sauce, and garnishes like shrimp, chicharon, eggs, and green onions.

Lumpia

Lumpia is a Filipino appetizer of thin spring roll wrappers filled with a flavorful mix of vegetables, meat, and spices, then deep-fried to crispy perfection.

Desserts

Abukado Lamaw

Iced avocado with condensed milk is a creamy and refreshing dessert made by blending ripe avocados with condensed milk, served over crushed ice.

Leche Flan

Leche flan is a rich and creamy caramel custard dessert popular in Filipino cuisine, made with eggs, condensed milk, and caramelized sugar.

Halo-halo

Halo-halo is a Filipino dessert with layers of sweet ingredients, including crushed ice, fruits, beans, jellies, topped with milk and toppings.

Cassava Cake

Cassava cake is a moist and sweet Filipino dessert made with grated cassava, coconut milk, and condensed milk, baked to perfection.
